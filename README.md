# RiskSense-AI
Predicting Risk. Protecting Earnings

THE STORY BEHIND RISKSENSE AI :
------------------------------
Every day, delivery riders step out to earn their livelihood. Unlike traditional jobs, their income depends completely on daily work. On a normal day, they earn steadily. But when conditions like heavy rain, extreme heat, or pollution occur, working becomes unsafe. In such situations, they are forced to stop working — and their income drops to zero. However, their expenses don’t stop. Rent, food, and family responsibilities continue. This creates a real problem: There is no system that protects their income during unavoidable disruptions.
RiskSense AI is designed to solve this gap.

WHAT IS RISKSENSE AI ?
----------------------
RiskSense AI is an AI-powered income protection system for delivery workers.
It works on a simple principle:
- if a rider cannot work due to real-world risks, they should still receive financial support.
- Instead of manual claims, the system automatically detects such situations and provides instant payouts.

UNDERSTANDING THE USER
----------------------
Consider a delivery rider who depends on daily earnings.
•	On normal days → steady income
•	On risky days → no income
What they need is not complex insurance, but a simple and reliable safety system that supports them when they genuinely cannot work.

CORE PROBLEM :
--------------
Delivery workers face:
•	Income loss due to weather and environmental conditions
•	Unsafe working situations
•	No real-time financial protection
•	Slow and impractical traditional insurance systems

SOLUTION APPROACH :
-------------------
RiskSense AI uses a parametric protection model. Instead of manual claim processing, the system works on predefined conditions.
- When a condition is met → payout is triggered automatically. This removes paperwork, delays, and manual approvals.

SYSTEM WORKFLOW :
-----------------
1.	User subscribes to a weekly membership plan
2.	System continuously monitors:
    - Weather conditions
    - Temperature levels
    - Air Quality Index
3.	If risk thresholds are crossed:
    - Rider activity is verified
4.	If impact is confirmed:
    - Instant payout is triggered
5.	Otherwise:
    - Monitoring continues

PARAMETRIC TRIGGERS :
---------------------
Payouts are triggered based on predefined conditions such as:
•	Heavy rainfall above a threshold
•	Extreme temperature levels
•	High pollution (AQI) levels
These triggers ensure:
•	Fast decision-making
•	No human dependency
•	Transparent execution

WEEKLY MEMBERSHIP MODEL :
-------------------------
RiskSense AI follows a weekly subscription model.
•	Users pay a small weekly premium
•	Premium is based on location risk and conditions

Why weekly?
•	Matches daily earning patterns
•	Affordable and flexible
•	Easy to manage
When disruption occurs, instant compensation is provided.

ROLE OF AI :
-----------
AI powers the intelligence of the system:
•	Risk Prediction: Identifies high-risk days using data
•	Dynamic Pricing: Adjusts premiums based on risk
•	Fraud Detection: Detects fake activity or location misuse
•	Continuous Learning: Improves accuracy over time

OUR GOAL :
----------
To build a system where no worker loses income due to conditions beyond their control.
 - RiskSense AI is more than a technical solution.
 - It provides delivery workers with security, confidence, and peace of mind during uncertain situations.

Finally , we are “Predicting risks early, protecting your earnings always.”
